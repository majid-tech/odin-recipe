Fajita Recipe

Description

In Tex-Mex cuisine, is any stripped grilled meat with stripped peppers and

onions that is usually served on a flour or corn tortilla.

The term originally referred to skirt steak, the cut of beef first used in the dish.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken.
  • Steak.
  • Cheese.
  • Salsa.
  • Guacamole.
  • Sour Cream.
  • Bell Peppers.
  • Onions.

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together 1‚ÅÑ2 cup oil, lime juice, cumin, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then add to bowl and toss to coat.
  3. Let marinate in the fridge at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours.
  4. When ready to cook, heat remaining tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes per side.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice into strips.
  7. Add bell peppers and onion to skillet and cook until soft, 5 minutes.
  8. Add chicken and toss until combined. Serve with tortillas
